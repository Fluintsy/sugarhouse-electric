Our Services

Professional electrical solutions for every need

🏠

Residential Electrical

Complete home electrical services including new construction wiring, rewiring, panel upgrades, outlet and switch installation, ceiling fan installation, and whole-home surge protection. We ensure your home's electrical system is safe and up to code.

🏢

Commercial Electrical

Full-scale electrical solutions for offices, retail spaces, restaurants, warehouses, and commercial facilities. We handle tenant improvements, new construction, lighting retrofits, and code compliance upgrades for businesses of all sizes.

🚨

Emergency Services

24/7 emergency electrical service for urgent situations including power outages, electrical fires, sparking outlets, and downed lines. We respond quickly throughout the Wasatch Front to keep your home or business safe.

⚡

Panel Upgrades

Upgrade your electrical panel from outdated fuse boxes to modern circuit breaker panels. We handle 100-amp to 400-amp upgrades to meet the demands of modern appliances, EV chargers, and home additions.

💡

Lighting Installation

Interior and exterior lighting design and installation including recessed lighting, landscape lighting, security lighting, LED retrofits, and smart lighting systems for homes and businesses.

🔌

Electrical Inspections

Comprehensive electrical inspections for home buyers, sellers, and property owners. We identify potential hazards, code violations, and provide detailed reports with recommended repairs.
