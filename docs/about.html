About Sugar House Electric

Your trusted local electrician since 2009

Built on Integrity, Quality, and Service

Sugar House Electric has been serving the Wasatch Front community since 2009. We started as a small, family-oriented electrical contractor and have grown into one of the area's most trusted electrical service providers.

Our business philosophy is focused on achieving customer satisfaction by exceeding customer expectations. We believe in doing the job right, the first time, every time.

Every member of our team is a licensed, trained professional who takes pride in their work. We treat every home and business as if it were our own, ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety on every project.
